## 📋 **Judge Q&A Prep**

### **Technical Questions:**
- **"How do you measure adaptability?"** → *"We analyze 4 key signals: recent hackathon participation (40 points), emerging skill adoption (25 points), continuous certification (20 points), and recency of learning activity (15 points). Each weighted based on predictive value."*

- **"What's your AI architecture?"** → *"Three specialized LangGraph agents: Profile Analyzer extracts adaptability signals, Job Matcher weighs role requirements, Outreach Coordinator personalizes communication. All backed by multi-tenant SQLite with real-time analytics."*

- **"How do you handle bias?"** → *"We focus on behavioral signals (learning, experimentation, growth) rather than demographic markers. Our scoring is transparent and auditable — every decision includes reasoning."*

### **Business Questions:**
- **"What's your competitive advantage?"** → *"We're the only platform measuring adaptability. Everyone else optimizes for keyword matching — we optimize for future potential."*

- **"How do you scale?"** → *"Multi-tenant architecture supports unlimited companies. ATS integrations mean zero workflow disruption. Each agent can process 1000+ profiles/hour."*

- **"What's your market size?"** → *"$200B global recruitment market, with 40% of companies reporting 'skills mismatch' as their #1 hiring challenge. We solve the mismatch by predicting learning ability."*
