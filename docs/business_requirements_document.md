# Business Requirements Document: AI Recruitment Platform

## Executive Summary
**Product Name**: Learning Velocity Recruitment Platform  
**Vision**: Transform tech recruiting by prioritizing learning velocity over static credentials  
**Target Market**: External SaaS for tech companies, recruiting agencies, and enterprises  
**Revenue Model**: Subscription-based with usage tiers  

## Business Objectives

### Primary Objectives
1. **Reduce Mis-hire Costs**: Target 40% reduction in expensive developer mis-hires ($240K+ average cost)
2. **Improve Time-to-Productivity**: 30% faster onboarding for learning-velocity hires
3. **Market Differentiation**: First platform to prioritize adaptability over static experience
4. **Revenue Target**: $2M ARR within 18 months of launch

### Success Metrics
- **Customer Acquisition**: 50+ enterprise customers in Year 1
- **User Engagement**: 80%+ monthly active usage rate
- **Hire Success Rate**: 25% improvement over traditional recruiting methods
- **Customer Retention**: 90%+ annual retention rate
- **Platform Usage**: 100% Complete.dev integration for competitive advantage

## Market Analysis

### Target Market Segments
1. **Primary**: Mid-size tech companies (100-1000 employees) with active hiring needs
2. **Secondary**: Technical recruiting agencies serving multiple clients
3. **Tertiary**: Enterprise companies (1000+ employees) with large engineering teams

### Market Size
- **TAM**: $15B global recruiting software market
- **SAM**: $3.2B technical recruiting segment
- **SOM**: $150M learning-focused recruiting niche (target 1% = $1.5M)

### Competitive Landscape
- **Traditional ATS**: Workday, Greenhouse, Lever (keyword-focused)
- **Technical Assessment**: HackerRank, Codility (static skill testing)
- **AI Recruiting**: Pymetrics, HireVue (personality-based)
- **Our Differentiation**: Only platform prioritizing learning velocity over credentials

## Technical Requirements

### Core Platform Requirements
1. **Multi-tenant SaaS Architecture**: Secure data isolation per customer
2. **API-First Design**: RESTful APIs for all core functions
3. **Real-time Processing**: Sub-5-second candidate assessment
4. **Scalability**: Handle 10,000+ concurrent assessments
5. **Data Security**: SOC 2 Type II compliance within 12 months

### Integration Requirements
1. **ATS Integration**: Greenhouse, Lever, Workday APIs
2. **LinkedIn Integration**: Profile extraction and analysis
3. **GitHub Integration**: Repository and contribution analysis
4. **Email/CRM**: Salesforce, HubSpot for outreach automation
5. **Analytics**: Tableau, PowerBI for reporting dashboards

### Performance Requirements
- **Uptime**: 99.9% availability SLA
- **Response Time**: <3 seconds for candidate scoring
- **Data Processing**: 1000+ profiles per hour batch processing
- **Concurrent Users**: Support 500+ simultaneous users

## Compliance & Legal Requirements

## Risk Assessment

### Technical Risks
- **AI Accuracy**: Risk of false positives/negatives in candidate assessment
- **Data Quality**: Dependency on LinkedIn and GitHub data availability
- **Scalability**: Platform performance under high load
- **Integration Complexity**: Third-party API reliability and changes

### Business Risks
- **Market Adoption**: Resistance to new hiring methodologies
- **Competition**: Large players entering learning-velocity space
- **Economic Downturn**: Reduced hiring budgets during recession
- **Regulatory Changes**: New employment law affecting AI recruiting

### Mitigation Strategies
- **Technical**: Comprehensive testing, fallback systems, performance monitoring
- **Business**: Strong customer validation, differentiated positioning, flexible pricing
- **Financial**: Diverse customer base, recurring revenue model, cost flexibility
- **Legal**: Proactive compliance, regular legal review, transparent practices
